Sara Melchionda

MA Candidate | Prospective PhD Researcher

I am a Master’s degree student in Public and Political Communication at the University of Turin. My research investigates the intersection of digital sociology and NLP, specifically focusing on the detection of epistemic distrust and narrative reasoning within online conspiratorial discourse. My current thesis project explores whether the erosion of institutional trust is accurately mapped by automated systems or requires human interpretative depth. I benchmark BERT vs. GPT-4o to decode vernacular internet slang, using large-scale datasets retrieved from Reddit.
